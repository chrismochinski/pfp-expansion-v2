We have been listening and we are ready to meet your needs in BIG ways. PurposeFull People 2.0 is coming June 30th as a FREE update for all users for your review and planning. Watch the 3:30 overview video below to learn more!

What to Expect

Grade Level Specific!

MORE content that is EASIER to navigate:

  • PreK-5 now has dedicated, individual curriculum
  • New site with everything at your fingertips
  • User-friendly organization
  • 36 unique weeks per grade level (4 weeks for each of 9 traits)
  • Built-in implementation plans
  • More projectable teaching tools to cut down on planning time

Easier to Navigate:

SEL + Character Deeper connection between Character Development and SEL:

  • Every lesson has 5 flexible parts: Start, Connect, Grow, Respond, and Exit
  • All content is sorted into Grade Levels and Traits
  • Each trait has 4 weeks of content
  • Full-screen interactive features like slides and games to support lesson content
  • Brand new Campus Resources section has everything in 1 place (sortable video library, book library, staff tools, morning announcements, assessments and more)

Explicit SEL Instruction:

  • Deeper connections between character traits and SEL
  • Explicit instruction on high-leverage social & emotional skills
  • Strong focus on conflict resolution, emotion regulation, and executive functioning skills
  • Targeted outcomes to help students Be Kind, Be Well, and Be Strong
